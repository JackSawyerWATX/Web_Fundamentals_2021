Sutliff 504C Aeromatic English

The base is nutty Burley and smooth black
Cavendish with a mellow top note. Then,
a measured amount of Cyprian Latakia is
added to add depth and a touch of leathery smokiness.

1 Item(s)

Sutliff African Queen

Besides the Burley and Virginia you might
expect, there's also some exotic and
somewhat spicy Turkish Smyrna. The blend
is finished with a combination of mellow flavors
that compliment the tobaccos and gives
Sutliff African Queen a wonderful room note.
